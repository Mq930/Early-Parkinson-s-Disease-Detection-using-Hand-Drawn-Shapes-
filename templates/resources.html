Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the drawing analysis test? ▼
Our AI-powered analysis has shown high accuracy in early detection, with a success rate of over 90% in clinical trials. However, it's important to note that this tool is meant for screening purposes and should not replace professional medical diagnosis.
What should I do if my test indicates potential symptoms? ▼
If your test results indicate potential symptoms, we recommend consulting with a healthcare provider for a thorough evaluation. Early detection and treatment can significantly improve outcomes.
How often should I take the test? ▼
We recommend taking the test every 6 months for regular monitoring. However, if you notice any changes in your drawing ability or hand movements, you can take it more frequently.
Is my data secure and private? ▼
Yes, we take your privacy seriously. All uploaded images are encrypted, analyzed securely, and deleted after analysis. We never share your personal information with third parties.
