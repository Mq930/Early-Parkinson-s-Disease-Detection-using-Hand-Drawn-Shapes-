Image Cropping Guide

Steps for Perfect Image Capture:

  1. Place your drawing on a flat surface with good lighting
  2. Hold your camera directly above the drawing
  3. Ensure the entire drawing is visible and centered
  4. Crop the image to remove excess background
  5. Check that the lines are clear and visible
